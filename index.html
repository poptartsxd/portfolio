About Me


Hi, I'm Victoria!

I am pursuing a masters degree in HCI at Georgia Tech, seeking a 2017 summer internship.

I studied Accounting as an undergrad but discovered a passion for technology and design towards the end of college (it was also much more fun than balancing debits and credits). Working as a consultant in the business world helped me develop a strong empathy for my users and clients. As a result, I decided to combine my passion for technology and empathy for others into creating a delightful user experience.

I love creating solutions that address a true user need. Coming into the design world has allowed me to follow this passion.

Whenever I find the time (ha!), I like to hike, rock climb, and take photos.
